5.4.2 problem 28

Starting from the fact given in the problem

P(|Y−μ|<1.96σ)≈0.95

P(−1.96σ<Y−μ<1.96σ)≈0.95

P(Y−1.96σ<μ<Y+1.96σ)≈0.95

P(μ∈(Y−1.96σ,Y+1.96σ))≈0.95

Therefore, the random interval that contains μ about 95% of the time is (Y−1.96σ,Y+1.96σ).
