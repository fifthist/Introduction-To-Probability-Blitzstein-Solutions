f(x)=xe−x2∕2

P(X≤a)=∫a0f(x)dx=∫a0xe−x2∕2dx=1−e−a2∕2

The quantile function Q, equal to the inverse of the CDF, is given by

Q(a)=√−2log(1−a)

(a)

P(X≤qj)=j4
