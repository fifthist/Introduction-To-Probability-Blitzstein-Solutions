1.5.1 problem 49

Let Ai be the event that i is never rolled for 1≤i≤6. The event of interested then is ⋃6i=1Ai.

By inclusion-exclusion, P(⋃6i=1Ai)=∑6i=1P(Ai)−∑1≤i<j≤6P(Ai∩Aj)+∑1≤i<j<k≤6P(Ai∩Aj∩Ak)−⋯−P(⋂6i=1Ai).

Now,

P(Ai)=5n6n=(56)n

P(Ai∩Aj)=4n6n=(46)n

P(Ai∩Aj∩Ak)=3n6n=(36)n

P(Ai∩Aj∩Ak∩Aw)=2n6n=(26)n

P(Ai∩Aj∩Ak∩Aw∩Az)=1n6n=(16)n

P(⋂6i=1Ai)=0

Thus, P(⋃6i=1Ai)=6(56)n−(62)(46)n+(63)(36)n−(64)(26)n+(65)(16)n
