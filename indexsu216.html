6.1.2 problem 2

Let k be the median, therefore P(X≥k)≥12 and P(X≤k)≥12, therefore P(X≤k)≤12.
Hence,

P(X≤k)=12F(k)=12k=ln(2)λ

where F is the CDF of X.

Let c be the mode, f be the PDF of X, since f(x) is decreasing for all x≥0 (f'(x)=−λ2e−λx) or is 0 otherwise, f(x) has its maxima when x=0, hence, the mode is 0.
