2.5.1 problem 57

(a)
Suppose C1 contains 7 green gummi bears and 8 red ones, M1 contains 1 green gummi bear and 2 red gummi bears, C2 contains 5 green gummi bears and no red gummi bears, M2 contains 12 green gummi bears and 5 red gummi bears.

The proportion of green gummi bears in C1 is 715, which is larger than that of M1, which is 13. The proportion of green gummi bears in C2 is 55, which is larger than that of M2, which is 1217. However, the proportion of green gummi bears in C1+C2 is 1220, which is less than that of M1+M2, which is 1320.
