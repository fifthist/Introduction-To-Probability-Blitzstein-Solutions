4.1.14 problem 15

(a)
Let X be the earnings by player B. Suppose B guesses a number j with probability bj. Then,

E(X)=100∑j=1jpjbj

To maximize E(X) then, B should set bj=1 for the j for which jpj is maximal. Since pj are known, this quantity is known.
