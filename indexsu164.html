4.3.8 problem 46

Let X∼Geom(113) be the number of non-aces before the first ace. Then, E(X)=13.

Let Y∼NHGeom(4,48,2) be the number of non-aces before the second ace is drawn. Then, E(X)=rbw+1=2∗485=19.2

Let Z=Y−X. Notice that Z represents the number of non-aces between the first and the second ace. E(Z)=E(Y)−E(X)=19.2−13=6.2.
