(a) The new PDF is,
\[g(x) = 2 F(x) f(x)\] 
\(g(x) \ge 0\) in the same range as f
\begin{flalign}
    \ri g(x) dx & = \ri 2 F(x) f(x) dx \\
    & = \ri d(F^2(x)) \\
    & = \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} F^2(x) - \lim_{x \rightarrow -\infty} F^2(x) \\
    & = 1 - 0
\end{flalign}

(b) The new PDF is,
\[g(X) = \frac{1}{2} (f(x) + f(-x))\]
\begin{flalign}
    \ri g(x) dx & = \frac{1}{2} \ri f(x) dx + \frac{1}{2} \ri f(x) dx \\
    & = 1
\end{flalign}
