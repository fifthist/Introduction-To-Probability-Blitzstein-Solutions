Take \(\text{Unif}(0, \frac{1}{2})\), \\
\[f(x) = \{ 2, x \in (0, \frac{1}{2})\}\]
\[\int_C f(x) dx = 1\] where \(C\) is the complete space.
Say, \(f(x) > 1\) in a given domain \(X\) \\
\(f(x) > 0\), so \(\int_D f(x) dx \le \int_C f(x) dx \) \\
Here \(\int_D 1 dx < \int_D f(x) dx\) \\
We can say, \(|D| < 1\)
